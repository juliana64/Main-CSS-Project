Plot of the movie

Chihiro is a stubborn ten-year-old girl who believes that the entire universe should bow to her wishes. On her way to their new home with her parents, the family takes a wrong turn and appears at the end of a mysterious dead end, where they come across a strange building with a long passage that leads to a ghostly town. Where a magnificent banquet awaits them.
